# Glossary

**TGBP** – ERC-20 stable token used for all trading.  
**EquityToken** – Per-company ERC-20 token representing listed stock.  
**Listing** – Process of deploying and registering a new stock token.  
**Limit Order** – Order executed at or better than a specific price.  
**Partial Fill** – Order partially matched; remainder stays active.  
**Epoch** – 3-minute window for reward computation.  
**FeePool** – Contract tracking volume & rewarding top trader.  
**Dividend** – TGBP payout declared to token holders.  
**Fresh/Stale** – UI badge indicating price data ≤ 60 s (old = stale).  
**OrderBookDEX** – On-chain order matching engine.  
**PortfolioAggregator** – Read-only view contract summarizing holdings.  
**AccessControl** – OpenZeppelin role-based authorization.  
**RPC_URL** – Blockchain endpoint (e.g., Hardhat localhost).  
**Admin Wallet** – Privileged address able to list stocks & declare dividends.  
**AirdropOnce()** – Function granting 1,000,000 TGBP on first signup.  
**Fee (ppm)** – Protocol fee of 0.0001 % charged to taker (trade initiator).  
**Epoch Reward** – 3 TGBP granted to top volume trader per epoch.
